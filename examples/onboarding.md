Use the Brand-Led UI Designer perspective.

Project:
An onboarding flow for a modern finance / crypto product.

Goal:
Help new users complete setup smoothly while making the experience feel clear, trustworthy, calm, and premium.

Platform:
Responsive web or mobile

This should feel:
- calm
- focused
- reassuring
- premium
- friction-light

Closer to:
- Apple-like setup calm
- Robinhood clarity
- minimal editorial product tone

Avoid:
- generic multi-step SaaS wizard feel
- cluttered instructional UI
- too many progress markers and panels
- overuse of cards and boxed sections
- playful fintech friendliness
- noisy illustrations or decorative visuals

What must stand out:
- the current step
- the primary action
- user confidence and progress
- the sense that the process is simple and manageable

What should stay quiet:
- secondary explanations
- edge-case information
- structural UI chrome
- optional supporting actions

Desired density:
- sparse to balanced

Constraints:
- Must support multiple steps
- Must work for users with low prior knowledge
- Should reduce anxiety during account / identity / setup tasks
- Must remain clear on mobile
- Accessibility should meet WCAG AA

What I need from you:
Give me one strong onboarding-flow direction, including:
1. Visual intent
2. Why it feels premium and reassuring
3. Hierarchy strategy step by step
4. Brand expression beyond accent color
5. UI foundations
6. Recommendations for progress, forms, and action structure
7. What should be removed or made quieter
8. Anti-generic review

Important:
The experience should feel guided, not over-managed.
Use spacing and hierarchy to reduce anxiety instead of adding more explanatory UI.
